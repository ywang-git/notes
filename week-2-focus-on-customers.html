Week 2: Focus on Customers

Learn

customer obsessed culture

focus more on customers than competitors

The Amazon Flywheel

Virtuous Cycle

sellers -> selection -> customer experience -> traffic -> sellers customer experience -> growth -> low cost structure -> lower price -> customer experience

Invent and Simplify

do not either or thinking, try to come up with solution to get both listen to customers, invent on their behalf

Think

  • who are your customers?
  • what do your customers need, expect, and want?
  • are you wowing your customers by delivering above their expectations? if not, what can you do about it
  • does your team understand who is the customer and the impact they have on their lives?

Apply

  • identify key customers
  • schedule meetings: pain points and priorities
  • questions
    • what is your history with my team?
    • what level of service does my team provide you?
    • any urgent or critical needs?
    • what are your pain points?
  • external: surveys / reviews
  • findings:
    • recurring theme?
    • action items to improve customer experience?
    • what needs to happen long-term to wow your customers?
