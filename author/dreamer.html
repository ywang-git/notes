Week 3: Set and Communicate Purpose and Direction

having clear purpose and direction - customer obsession is critical - ensure your team understand the end goal (purpose) - significant impact on customers - clear direction on how to get there

Course

1. Define Your Purpose

Intro

Alexa example

omni presens -> StarTrek computer BHAG: Big / Hairy / Audacious / Goal Purpose: Like a Whack-a-mole the "never green goal" Direction: Actions that come from Working Backwards from Goal

Why it matters

purpose -> why? direction -> how?

Practice Defining Your Purpose

Purpose and Direction
  • Clarity: clarity about where they are going and why
  • Challenge: the work challenges and stretches the individuals on the team and the team as a group
  • Consequence: the work of the team matters, real consequence to the customer and to the business

direction and purpose are critical contributors to team performance

"in order to drive performance, the purpose must include an element of winning, coming first, transforming or revolutionizing, or simply accomplishing something that really matters."

purpose starts at the top, but grows from team clarity - focus on the team direction: - ensuring they have clarity with leadership above them - ensuring that team members are clear about directions - goals, outcomes, metrics and visions mistake: underestimate what it takes for team members to get clear: real business or organizational vision

allow team members to handle the 'hows'

mistake: get too involved in deciding how goals and outcomes should be achieved
