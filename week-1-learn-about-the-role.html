Week 1: Learn about the role

  • drive business like an owner
  • deliver exceptional solutions
  • guide the careers of staff
  • dive deep
  • be a voice for customer
  • focus on operational excellence
  • start with people, deeply invested in team
  • operate at high levels
  • connected to details
  • interested in what the team is doing

avoiding new managers pitfalls

  • deliver thru others
  • understand where to intervene and help
  • overlook performance group (top / bottom)
  • micromanage vs totally hands off
  • attracting / develop new talent

SDM self-assessment

  • prioritizing / solving complex problems / lead team
  • positive team environment
  • managing team / engage people / make sure on track
  • forward-thinking / strategic / effectively communicate / inspire vision in others
  • collaborate peers and other teams
  • determine process / technology
  • deep technical understanding
  • VJT (virtual job tryout) GIST
    • go above and beyond
    • juggle multiple priority
    • coach other people to improve performance
    • fast pace environment
    • take lead
    • influence others opinion

insider's guide

work backwards

be an owner

  • team mission, tenets and deliverables
  • product definition / project management
  • design and architecture
  • quality
  • operational excellence
  • available and latency
  • customer and financial metrics
  • scalability and efficiency
  • staffing: recruiting and retention
  • employee development, performance management, promotions
  • budget

own dependencies and escalations

OE

  • create operational metrics
  • set SLA
  • review measurement weekly
  • review operational metrics to meet SLA

data

  • baseline with relative metrics
  • percentiles (rather than average
  • analyze metrics at four 9s / five 9s
  • look at whole distribution
  • track everything
